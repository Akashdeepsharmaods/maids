Vacation Rental

Cleaning Guide

January 13, 2024

When you think about your summer vacation rental, you’re probably thinking of marketing your property, decorating your summer rental, and other ways to keep your place booked all summer long. Whether it’s good ol’ word of mouth or a digital review, how favorable guests rate your summer rental will often have to do with your vacation rental turnover cleaning ability.

To get five stars for your vacation rental, you have to treat it like a luxury five-star hotel—particularly when it comes to rental cleaning. Any dirt, grime, stains or even a few hairs left behind in sensitive areas such as beds, toilets, showers and kitchens will offend guests and likely affect their review.

Whether you clean your vacation rental yourself or use our vacation rental cleaning services, make sure high standards are set from the beginning and a deep, thorough cleaning is done every single time a guest departs.

How Vacation Rental Cleaning is Different From a Typical House Cleaning

When guests book a stay in your summer rental, they expect it to be immaculately clean. Some may even assume the property is cleaned by professional vacation property cleaners. Don’t give them any reason to doubt the place is anything but perfect.

Make your rental unit feel like home, but with a level of cleaning a step above a typical house cleaning. Here are just some tasks you can expect from professional vacation rental cleaning services:

  • Cleaning the linens used by previous guests
  • Cleaning surfaces, including disinfecting commonly touched areas like door handles
  • Restocking toilet paper, paper towels, cleaning products and other essentials
  • Inspecting for damage
  • Testing electronics and appliances
  • Sweeping, mopping, vacuuming, dusting, scrubbing and disinfecting room by room

Remember, you may not see perfectly cleaning rental property as a big deal, but your guests do. They want it to be exciting and new, but they still want it to feel like their own home away from home. If you want your guests to be impressed with your vacation rental cleaning, you better be ready to work or call in the pros.

Can I Do a Vacation Rental Cleaning Myself?

While a comprehensive vacation rental cleaning checklist can help you cover all the bases if you are cleaning your rental yourself, there are other factors to consider. Doing your own cleaning reduces costs and should increase your income. After all, the goal of owning a vacation rental is to make a profit, but it’s important to evaluate the true savings.

If you don’t live in the same area as your summer rental or lack the time to maintain and clean your place, then doing the cleaning yourself may not be an attractive option. Even if you are close by and have some free time, can you really commit to deep cleaning the entire vacation rental weekly and sometimes more often?

During the summer rental season, you hopefully have back-to-back bookings. But these quick turns mean guests will typically check out around 10 a.m. and the next guests check-in around 4 p.m. That gives you approximately six hours to clean your summer rental from top to bottom to get it in pristine condition.

A cleaning service may be a better option. A reputable residential cleaning service can save you time, money and negative reviews as long as you pick the right one. To find a housekeeping service that provides the thorough cleaning and dependability you need, here are some things to look for:

  • Protect your rental, guests and reputation by choosing a cleaning service that is licensed, insured and bonded.
  • Make sure any housekeeping service you are considering offers a range of services so that you will always have the cleaning resources you need.
  • Choose a maid service that is well-known and highly regarded, preferably with verified reviews from satisfied customers.
  • Be sure to choose a cleaning service that has proven they can clean quickly and efficiently with every service.

Remember, you only have one shot at impressing new guests and anything you miss during cleaning could mean a less than favorable rating and review.

Why Choose Vacation Rental Cleaning Services From The Maids

Today, turning a home into a vacation rental is big business and for good reason. By renting, homeowners get the ability to earn extra income on an otherwise empty space and guests receive a more comfortable living experience while on vacation or traveling for work.

One of the top standards for providing a space for guests is to make sure it is clean, really clean! When the success of your vacation rental or Airbnb depends on reviews, you don’t want to leave anything to chance. You need to hire a professional vacation rental cleaning service.

And before you balk at the idea for cutting into your profits, consider the fact that your guests typically pay the cleaning fee. Vacation renters expect to be charged a standard cleaning fee, so as long as the fee is on par with market prices, it should fit right in as a line item.
